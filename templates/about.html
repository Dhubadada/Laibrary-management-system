Our History

Founded in 1985 with just 500 books in a small community center, Trust Library has grown into one of the region's most comprehensive knowledge hubs.

Our digital transformation began in 2010, allowing us to serve patrons beyond our physical walls. Today, we offer over 500,000 physical items and 200,000 digital resources.

In 2020, we launched our AI-powered recommendation system and mobile app, making personalized reading suggestions available to all members.

Our newest initiative, the "Global Classroom" program, connects learners with international authors and experts through virtual events.
